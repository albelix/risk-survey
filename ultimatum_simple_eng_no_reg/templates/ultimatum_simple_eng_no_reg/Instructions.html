Instructions

You are randomly paired with another experimental participant from this session. You will not know who this participant is, and neither will he/she know who you are.

There are two roles in this experiment: Participant 1 and Participant 2. . All participants will be randomly assigned to one of these roles. Before you start the experiment, all of you will learn your roles.

After all participants learn their roles, Participant 1 receives the initial amount in ECU. Participant 1 decides how many ECU (s)he decides to transfer to Participant 2.

Participant 2 may agree with this proposal of Participant 1, in which case both participants receive the shares proposed by Participant 1. If Participant 2 does ot agree with this proposal of Participant 1 --- both participants receive 0.
